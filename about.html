    About Memory Match Game

    There are 9 pairs of matching cards faced down. The objective is to click on 2 at a time to find the matching pairs. As they are matched, they will stay face up. If you click on two that do not match, it will turn them face down so you can continue to find the rest of the matching pairs. Reveal all face down cards and you will win.
